French Crepes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup of peanuts

Kitchen Equipment

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Whisk
  • 8" skillet
  • Stove
  • Ladle

Directions

  1. Mix water and milk in bowl
  2. whisk eggs into the milk and water mix
  3. slowly add flour until all gone and smooth consitancy
  4. add salt and oil
  5. heat skillet on stove at medium heat
  6. Ladle batter into pan tiliting batter to cover entire pan base
  7. flip when edges curl away from pan
  8. remove when golden brown repeat until batter is gone
  9. Serve with sugar, lemon, butter, and salt
